Precent of Population Enroled in School in US

Percent of individuals within the population that are enrolled in some form of education in the United States by county, represented using a choropleth map.

Normal Storage of US Dams

Spatial distribution of the normal storage, in acre-feet, of dams in the contiguous United States in 2018, represented using proportional symbol mapping.

Median Housing Value and % Black Population in Georgia

This map shows the intersection of median housing value and % Black population for each census tract in Georgia, represented using a bivariate color scheme.

Yearly Total Precipitation in Iowa

This map shows average precpitation in Iowa in 2018. This data is represented using an isarithmic where precipitation values confined to ranges and grouped spatialy regardless of enumeration units, such as counties or census tracts.

Well, Well, Well: An Investigation of Health Risks Associated With Nitrate in Private Wells in Iowa

This study aims to explore and visualize health risks associated with exposure to elevated Nitrate levels in private wells Iowa and define areas of elevated risk across the state.

Migration of Families within U.S. (1887-1924)

This map illustrates the Migration of Families withing the U.S. from 1887 until 1924. There are three variables visualized in this map, including: the number of families migrating (flows), number of total family migration events (nodes), and net family migration (regions), created using Flow Mapper.
