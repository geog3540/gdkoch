Grace Koch Environmnetal Science Student

I am a Junior studying Environmental Science at the University of Iowa. Broadly, I am interested in exploring environmental justice in commercial and natural water systems. Currently, my research revolves around water quality in Kiosks across the state of Iowa and the relations between real and perceived government failure with Dr. Samantha Zuhlke. Additionally, we also research public policy outcomes in Iowa small towns, utilizing google street view, this project is funded by National Geographic. Finally, I also study prairie plants in relation to soil Haney Score at Ashton Prairie Living Laboratories. I currently work as an Operator at the Iowa City Water Plant. I previously worked in the Water Quality Division at the Iowa Department of Natural Resources, collecting data on surface water quality across the state.
